Artisan Marghertia Pizza

A pizza that is typically made with high-quality, fresh ingredients and baked in a wood-fired oven to achieve a crispy crust and unique flavor. It is often hand-stretched to form a thin and crispy base, and topped with ingredients such as fresh vegetables, meats, and cheeses. The term "artisan" refers to the traditional, handmade methods used in its preparation, and the focus on using premium, locally-sourced ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Garlic clove
  • 5 ounces crushed fire roasted tomatoes or high quality organic canned tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 to 1/3 cup pizza sauce
  • Scant ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¾ cup shredded cheese or 2 to 3 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • A few fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Make the pizza dough
  2. Place a pizza stone in the oven and preheat to 500°F
  3. Make the pizza sauce

  5. Bake the pizza
  6. Spread a thin layer of the pizza sauce over the dough,
  7. Use the pizza peel to carefully transfer the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone.
  8. Allow the pizza to cool for a minute or two before adding the basil on top
